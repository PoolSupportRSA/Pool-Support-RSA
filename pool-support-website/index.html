Who We Are & What We Do

Pool Support is a trusted South African pool service company established in 2017, dedicated to keeping swimming pools clean, safe, and fully operational.

We provide reliable maintenance, repairs, refurbishments, and pool system upgrades across Cape Town, Pretoria, and Rustenburg.

  • Pool maintenance & cleaning
  • Pool repairs & servicing
  • Pump repairs & replacements
  • Pool heating installations
  • Pool refurbishments & upgrades
  • Water balancing & treatment
  • Emergency pool support
